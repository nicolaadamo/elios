Our Story is
Our Family

After teaching for 30 years in Sicilian high schools, I decided to devote all my time to farming my family’s land following the tradition of my family that for centuries has lived in contact with nature.

Thus I have experimented new sustainable ways of growing vines and olive trees, using only natural means, to produce organic products.

We've gone far, and today

together with my two sons Roberto and Nicola we run our Farm
