Back to nature...& Beekeeping: Nicola

Back to nature...& Beekeeping: Nicola

I got the MSc in Computer Engineering in 2009 and in the following years I worked as a pure Software designer and developer. Although I always loved my work, every year I felt more and more the need to do things to approach nature more.

An innovative WINE Project: Guido

An innovative WINE Project: Guido

Guido is one of our best friend and he joined us in ELIOS proposing a great WINE Project idea: making a Wine that is DIFFERENT, A NATURAL WINE.

In his 6 years work experience as a WINEMAKER Consultant, he matured the awareness that Wine shouldn't be made using strange substances or as an artificial product; instead the man should, from his point of view, accompany natural wine making processes without intrusive and invasive intervention.

THIS makes the wines really TYPICAL and CHARACTERISTIC of the territory from which grapes come.

The idea of making natural wines comes from deep respect we have for nature and the concept of following the Nature makes us feel part of it. That's why we married Guido's idea.

TODAY he is responsible of following the wine making process and managing customers relationships.
